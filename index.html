About BrandXcel

Brand Xcel is an outcome of extensive consumer research offering a deeper consumer connection and understanding (20,000 respondents across cities, including Rural India) along with honouring and featuring the Top Brands in India. We as a research and marketing fraternity acknowledge that the uncertainty due to the pandemic and other factors has disrupted consumers normal lives, which has implications for their relationship with brands. The evolved basket of brands that consumers now find relevant, offers an opportunity to understand the underlining traits, that make these brands meaningful. This is what we have attempted to cover, with the same complemented by leading industry brand custodians.

The key highlights of the book are the brand rankings based on consumer choice and the impact that it had on their life, the depiction of the blurring of lines between the urban and rural as well as the trends that have stayed and those that have fizzled out.
